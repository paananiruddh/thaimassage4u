What to include in your first booking message

A good first message saves time for both sides. You do not need to write much — just include the details that make a booking conversation clear.

Use this short checklist

  • Service preference: relaxation, deep tissue, or blended
  • Preferred time window between 9 am and 6 pm
  • Suburb or location page that best matches you
  • Pressure or comfort notes

Example short SMS

"Hi, I’m looking for a blended session this week. Preferably between 2 pm and 5 pm, and South Yarra is closest for me. Moderate pressure preferred."

Example short email

"Hello, I’d like to enquire about a deep tissue booking. My preferred time is around midday, and Richmond is my nearest area. I prefer moderate-to-firm pressure in upper back and shoulders."

Common mistakes to avoid

  • Sending a message without preferred timing
  • Leaving out service preference entirely
  • Overloading the first message with unrelated details
