How to use the Locations page to choose the right suburb page

The Locations page works best as a quick comparison tool. You can choose a suburb page by booking style, then move straight to contact when you are ready.

Start with your booking style

If you prefer a calmer, more structured approach, Armadale or Toorak may be useful starting points. If you want faster decision support, South Yarra or Melbourne CBD may be a better fit.

Choose by session priority

Richmond content focuses on targeted planning language, while Brighton leans more comfort-first. St Kilda is positioned for flexible planning with clear next steps.

Move from comparison to action

After choosing a suburb page, use that page’s enquiry checklist and then contact via SMS or email with your service preference and timing window.

FAQ

Do I need to read every suburb page?

No. Start with one or two that best match your booking style, then proceed to contact.

Can I still choose a different suburb page later?

Yes. The suburb pages are comparison tools; they are there to make your decision easier, not restrictive.
