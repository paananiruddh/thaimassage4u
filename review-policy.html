Policy

Review policy

This directory does not import or republish third-party reviews. Listings are based on verifiable business facts and manual editorial review.

What is not included

  • No Google review scraping or review summaries
  • No copied testimonial text from external platforms
  • No star ratings copied from third-party sites

What can be included

  • Service categories explicitly shown on business websites
  • Booking style and contact methods shown publicly
  • Suburb and city coverage confirmed by source pages

Correction workflow

Owners can request corrections via Claim Listing. Updates are manually checked before publication in static data files.
