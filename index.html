Frequently Asked Questions About Thai Massage Melbourne

Common questions from our clients about massage therapy, booking, and what to expect.

What is Thai massage and how is it different from other massage types?

Thai massage is a traditional healing practice that combines assisted stretching, acupressure, and rhythmic compression. Unlike oil-based massages, traditional Thai massage is performed fully clothed on a floor mat, making it more active and dynamic than Western massage styles.

Do I need to book ahead?

Yes, booking ahead is recommended to secure your preferred time slot and therapist. You can book online through our website or call us directly.

What should I wear to a Thai massage appointment?

For traditional Thai massage, wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. For oil-based massage services, you'll be provided with appropriate draping for your privacy and comfort.

How long do massage sessions typically last?

Session lengths vary from 45 minutes to 90 minutes depending on the service. Most clients choose 60-minute sessions for a comprehensive treatment experience.

Is Thai massage suitable for everyone?

Thai massage is generally safe for most people, but please inform your therapist of any medical conditions, injuries, or concerns before your session. Pregnant women and those with certain health conditions should consult their doctor first.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, and EFTPOS. Payment is taken at the end of your session.
