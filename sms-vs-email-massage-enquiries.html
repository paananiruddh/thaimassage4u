SMS vs email for massage enquiries: which should you use?

Both channels are available, but they suit different situations. This guide helps you choose the faster or clearer path based on what you need.

When SMS is usually the better option

SMS is useful when you want a concise booking request and quick back-and-forth. It is best for short messages with clear basics: service type, preferred time window, and suburb.

When email is usually better

Email is useful when you want to share more detail in one message, such as service preference, scheduling notes, and comfort preferences.

What to include either way

  • Preferred service style
  • Preferred time between 9 am and 6 pm
  • Preferred suburb page or area focus
  • Any comfort preferences

Keep your first message short and practical

A simple, clear message usually gets better results than a long explanation. You can always add more details after the first reply.
