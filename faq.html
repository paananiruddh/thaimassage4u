Practical questions

Frequently asked questions

These answers are designed to help you choose a service, prepare for your session, and contact us with the right details.

How should someone choose between the services?

If your priority is to unwind, start with relaxation. If you have persistent stiffness in specific areas, deep tissue is usually a better fit. If you want both, choose a blended session.

Do I need to know exactly what pressure I want?

No. You can describe comfort preferences in simple terms (lighter, moderate, or firmer). Pressure should always be adjusted based on feedback during the session.

What should I share when booking?

Your preferred service type, suburb, day/time preference, and any comfort notes help us guide you to the right option quickly.

Which Melbourne areas are covered?

Current focus areas are listed on the Locations page, including Armadale, Toorak, South Yarra, Richmond, Melbourne CBD, Brighton, and St Kilda.

What are your opening hours and payment methods?

Opening hours are 9 am to 6 pm, and accepted payment methods are Cash or PayID.

Can massage outcomes be guaranteed?

No. Sessions are wellness-focused, and individual responses vary. Clear communication about comfort and expectations is part of the booking process.
