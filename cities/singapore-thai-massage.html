Frequently asked questions

What is the usual price for Thai massage in Singapore?

In Singapore, most customers will see prices in the range of S$70 to S$150 per hour, depending on session length, location, fit-out, and whether the business positions itself as a spa or a practical massage studio.

How do I choose a good Thai massage place in Singapore?

Start with cleanliness, recent customer reviews, clear pricing, professional communication, and realistic treatment descriptions. In Singapore, businesses that explain session length, style, and booking rules clearly are usually easier to trust.

Is Thai massage in Singapore good for stress and stiffness?

Thai massage is commonly chosen by people who want stretching, pressure work, and general body relaxation. Many visitors in Singapore look for it after long flights, desk work, gym sessions, or busy work weeks.

Do I need to book ahead in Singapore?

Booking ahead is usually the safer option in Singapore, especially in busy districts, evenings, weekends, and holiday periods. Walk-ins can work, but popular times often fill quickly.
