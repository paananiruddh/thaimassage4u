From search to booking: a simple path for first-time visitors

If you are visiting for the first time, this path helps you move through the site in a practical order without reading unnecessary pages.

Step 1: choose a service direction

Start on the Services page and decide whether your priority is relaxation, targeted tension work, or a blended option.

Step 2: choose a suburb page

Use the Locations page to pick one suburb page that matches your booking style. You do not need to review all suburbs.

Step 3: prepare your first message

Include service preference, preferred time window (9 am to 6 pm), and comfort notes. Keep it concise.

Step 4: contact by SMS or email

Use Contact to send your message. SMS is preferred; email is available if you want more detail in one message.

Step 5: confirm practical details

Check payment method (Cash or PayID) and ask for any final booking details at confirmation stage.
