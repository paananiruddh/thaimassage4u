Common booking questions, answered in plain language

This article covers common booking questions in one place so you can move from research to enquiry with less uncertainty.

Which service should I choose?

Choose based on your main goal: unwind, focused tension work, or a blend of both.

Do I need exact pressure terminology?

No. Simple descriptions such as lighter, moderate, or firmer are enough to start.

What contact method should I use?

SMS is preferred, and email is also available when you want to include more details.

What are current hours and payment methods?

Opening hours are 9 am to 6 pm. Payment methods accepted are Cash or PayID.

Are outcomes guaranteed?

No. Sessions are wellness-focused, and responses vary by person and context.
