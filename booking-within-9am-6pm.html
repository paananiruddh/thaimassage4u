How to plan a booking within 9 am to 6 pm hours

With fixed operating hours, planning your enquiry in time windows can make booking conversations simpler and faster.

Use time windows, not single times

Instead of requesting one exact time only, send a preferred window (for example, early afternoon). This gives more flexibility while keeping your request specific.

Match session type to your day

If your day is packed, a clear service preference helps reduce back-and-forth. If you are unsure, say that you are deciding between two options.

Practical planning checklist

  • Preferred service type
  • One or two suitable windows between 9 am and 6 pm
  • Preferred contact channel: SMS or email

If your schedule changes

Send a short follow-up message with your new window and keep the rest of your details the same. Consistent details make updates easier to process.
