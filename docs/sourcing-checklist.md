# Manual Sourcing Checklist (Business Website Only)

## Allowed sources
- Official business website pages
- Official contact/about pages on the same domain
- Owner-submitted updates verifiable on official site

## Required verification steps
1. Confirm business name from official website.
2. Confirm city and suburb coverage.
3. Confirm category from official wording.
4. Confirm booking style.
5. Capture source URL.
6. Record verification date in `source_checked_date`.
7. Exclude unsupported claims.

## Quality checks before publish
- No fabricated data fields
- No medical-outcome guarantees
- No suggestive wording
- Description is factual and concise
- Filters are populated consistently
