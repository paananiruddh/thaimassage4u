Editorial policy

This directory is manually populated and updated from business websites and owner submissions. It does not scrape Google Search, Google Maps, or Google reviews.

Source standards

  • Use official business websites as primary source
  • Use owner-submitted updates only when verifiable
  • Record source URL and verification date for each listing
  • Do not invent pricing, service categories, or booking methods

Content boundaries

  • No copied third-party review text
  • No medical-outcome guarantees
  • No suggestive or ambiguous service framing
  • Corrections are made through manual review workflow
