Editorial policy

This directory is manually populated from official business websites and owner submissions that can be verified. It does not scrape third-party platforms.

Source standards

  • Use official business websites as the primary source
  • Use owner-submitted updates only when verifiable
  • Record source URL and verification date for each listing
  • Do not invent pricing, categories, contact details, or hours

Content boundaries

  • No copied third-party review text or ratings
  • No medical-outcome guarantees or treatment promises
  • No suggestive, ambiguous, or sexualized service framing
  • Corrections are handled through manual review workflow
