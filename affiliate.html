Affiliate disclosure

This page explains how referral links are disclosed if they are used. The aim is simple: clear language so readers understand when a recommendation may include a commission.

How referral links are handled

If referral links are used, they are kept relevant to the page topic and clearly disclosed. The goal is to keep recommendations useful and transparent, not distracting.

This page exists so readers can understand disclosure terms before clicking any referral links.

Disclosure statement

Some pages may include referral links. If you click one and later purchase or book, the site may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. Referral links are only used when they are relevant to the page content.

Good affiliate practice

  • Keep disclosures visible and plain-English
  • Separate editorial usefulness from pure monetisation
  • Only link where the fit is obvious
  • Preserve the local-service feel of the site
