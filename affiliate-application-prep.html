Affiliate application support

Affiliate application prep checklist and reusable answers

This page is a preparation guide only. It helps organise details commonly requested by affiliate programs and provides draft text that can be adapted for application forms.

Typical details requested in affiliate applications

  • Business/site name and primary website URL
  • Country and operating region
  • Main site purpose and audience
  • Traffic sources (for example search, direct, social, email)
  • Promotional methods (content reviews, guides, comparison pages)
  • Primary contact email and contact method
  • Disclosure policy URL
  • Privacy policy URL
  • Any social profiles used for promotion: [NEEDS INPUT]
  • Monthly traffic volume and engagement metrics: [NEEDS INPUT]

Reusable draft: business description

Draft answer: “Massage Services For You is a Melbourne-focused wellness information website. It helps visitors choose between massage service styles, compare selected suburb pages, and make enquiries using clear practical guidance.”

Reusable draft: traffic source

Draft answer: “Our content is structured for visitors who discover service and suburb guides, then move through internal links to FAQ and Contact pages. Confirmed channel mix percentages are [NEEDS INPUT].”

Reusable draft: promotional methods

Draft answer: “We publish practical, non-hyped informational content such as service selection guides, booking-preparation checklists, and suburb comparison pages. Any referral links are disclosed clearly on our affiliate disclosure page.”

Reusable draft: website purpose

Draft answer: “The website exists to help real visitors make better booking decisions through clear service explanations, suburb-specific guidance, and direct contact pathways.”
